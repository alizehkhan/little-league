Divisions & Fees

We are a developmental league that welcomes all skill levels. All registered players are assessed on their fielding, throwing, catching, and hitting skills. This is used to create balanced teams across each division.

Your baseball age is determined by your child's age on the AGE CUTOFF date.

Fees

Fees include the use of fields, equipment, practices, games, uniform, team photo and end of season trophy/medal. Fees are non-refundable.
